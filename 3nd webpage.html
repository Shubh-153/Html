Amazon

Amazon is a multinational technology company founded by Jeff bezos. It started as an online bookstore in 1994 and has since grown into one of the world s largest e-commerce and cloud computing companices.

The company is best known for its online retail platform, Amazon.com, which sells a vast range of products. Beyond e-commerce, amazon has diversified into several other key areas. Its most profiable business is amazon web services(AWS) , a leading provider pf cloud computing service.

Amazon also operates a subscripation service called Amazon prime , which offers benefits like free and fast shipping, as well as amazon prime, which offers benefits like free and fast shipping, as well as acess to its digital media platforms like Prime video and amazon music. Its influence extends to logistics, artifical intelligence (with alexa), and consumer electronics(with kindle and echo devices)
