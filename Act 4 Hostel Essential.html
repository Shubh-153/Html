Optional list
Mattress Topper:
A foam or padded topper can make a standard dorm mattress much more comfortable.
Small Rug:
Adds a touch of personality and warmth to a cold tile floor.
Comfy Chair:
A beanbag, foldable saucer chair, or small armchair is great for reading or having friends over.
Full-Length Mirror:
Useful for checking your outfit before heading out.
Blackout Curtains:
Essential if you're a light sleeper or enjoy sleeping in on weekends.
Whiteboard or Corkboard:
Perfect for reminders, to-do lists, or pinning up photos.
Small Safe:
To securely store important documents, extra cash, or valuables.
Bedside Shelf/Caddy:
A convenient spot to keep your phone, glasses, and a book within reach.
Lap Desk:
Allows you to comfortably use your laptop while sitting on your bed or a chair.
Coffee Maker or Electric Kettle:
A must-have for coffee or tea lovers to save money and time.
Blender:
Great for making smoothies for a quick and healthy breakfast.
Noise-Canceling Headphones:
A major upgrade for studying in noisy dorms or common areas.
Portable Charger/Power Bank:
Lifesaver for long days on campus when you can't find an outlet.
